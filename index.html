About

Jiangsu Dete Chemical is a fine chemical production and management company. We focus on product, we do research and development independently, and we supply raw materials for the special chemical industry. Not only can we meet the various requirements of customers, but also have the strength to assist customers from developing new products to industrial production, becoming a channel between fast-growing Chinese fine chemical industry and foreign markets.

OUR FACTORY

After years of domestic intensive cultivation, Jiangsu Dete Chemical has also fully participated in the real economy and has world-leading manufacturing bases as follows.

Jingjiang Factory

We produce and sell organic intermediates, pharmaceutical intermediates and fine chemicals. As the only manufacturer of ethylbenzyl chloride in the country and the largest production company in the world, it covers 80% of the global demand.

Penglai Factory

The company covers an area of about 100,000 square meters and is a high-tech enterprise with the largest production scale and the most complete product series in the research, development, production and sales of thermal materials.

The product WINCON-2 occupies more than 70% of the global market share and is currently the most widely used thermal paper coupler in the world. The company has the core technology leading the world. With its superior management level and style, professional market development team and reliable product quality, the company has always been at the forefront of the thermal material industry, which will further promote the development of my country′s specialty paper industry. Reducing the foreign dependence of some special papers in my country has broad development prospects.

Nantong Factory

The annual output of triazole products such as 5,000 tons of benzotriazole, 5,000 tons of methylbenzotriazole, 100 tons of pentamethylbenzotriazole, and 3,000 tons of bisphenol S. The market share of the products is stable and ranks high in the world, and the products are widely used in the automotive corrosion-inhibiting refrigerator and water treatment industries, as well as in the electronic chemical industry.
